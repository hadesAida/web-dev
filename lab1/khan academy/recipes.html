Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4 large
Pancetta 150g
Parmesan Cheese 100g
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water for 10 minutes or until al dente.

Step 2: In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated parmesan cheese. Season with black pepper.

Step 3: Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy, then toss with the cooked spaghetti.

Step 4: Quickly stir in the egg mixture, making sure the heat from the pasta cooks the eggs.

Step 5: Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

Source: BBC Good Food

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Cocoa Powder 50g
Sugar 200g
Eggs 2
Milk 200ml
Butter 100g
Baking Powder 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake tin.

Step 2: In a bowl, sift the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder.

Step 3: Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.

Step 4: Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

Step 5: Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk, mixing until smooth.

Step 6: Pour the batter into the tin and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Step 7: Allow the cake to cool before icing and serving.

Source: AllRecipes
